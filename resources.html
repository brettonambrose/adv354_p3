Educational Resources

A number of education resources exist for people interested in learning more about LAMP stacks and system administration. These range from enrichment programs to industry-recognized certifications. Courses are offered for free and premium rates. A strong foundation in the technological principals at the heart of what powers the Internet provides support for better and more efficient content development, improved UX, lower costs of support, and platform extensibility.

Despite the many options provided, one of the best ways to get started on a journey to system administration is simply "spinning-up" your own server.

Udemy

Courses offed by Udemy teach stack basics and serve as an excellent primer for anyone interested in system administration.
